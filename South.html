Overview of the Southern Region

The southern region of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is known for its natural diversity, combining green mountains, coastlines, and deserts. It includes major cities such as Abha, Khamis Mushait, Jazan, Najran, and Al Baha. The region is renowned for its cultural heritage, pleasant climate, and varied tourist attractions, making it a popular destination for visitors.
