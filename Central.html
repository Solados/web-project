Central Region
The heart of Saudi Arabia, the Central Region encompasses Riyadh, the modern capital; Al-Qassim with its fertile oases and agricultural heritage; and Hail with majestic mountains and ancient rock art. This region showcases Saudi identity through historic landmarks and warm hospitality.

Northern Region
The Northern Region spans Al-Jouf with ancient civilization and olive groves; Tabuk, gateway to historical sites; and Northern Borders with vast deserts and archaeological treasures. Experience ancient ruins and unique tribal heritage.

Western Region
Home to the holy cities, the Western Region includes Makkah, the spiritual heart of Islam; Madinah with sacred sites; Jeddah's historic Al-Balad and modern waterfront; and Taif's cool mountains and rose gardens. Experience spirituality, coastal beauty, and mountain retreats.

Southern Region
The mountainous South encompasses Asir with terraced farms and vibrant festivals; Jazan's tropical islands and cultural diversity; Najran's ancient architecture; and Al-Baha's stunning mountain vistas. Discover breathtaking landscapes and unique traditions.

Eastern Region
The Eastern Province, the kingdom's economic powerhouse, includes Dammam's modern center; Khobar's commercial hubs; Qatif's date palms and fishing traditions; and Al-Ahsa, a UNESCO site with the world's largest oasis. Experience progress, natural wonders, and rich heritage.
