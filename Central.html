Southern Region
The mountainous South encompasses Asir with terraced farms and vibrant festivals; Jazan's tropical islands and cultural diversity; Najran's ancient architecture; and Al-Baha's stunning mountain vistas. Discover breathtaking landscapes and unique traditions. Eastern Region
The Eastern Province, the kingdom's economic powerhouse, includes Dammam's modern center; Khobar's commercial hubs; Qatif's date palms and fishing traditions; and Al-Ahsa, a UNESCO site with the world's largest oasis. Experience progress, natural wonders, and rich heritage. Visitors can explore museums, festivals, historic sites, coastal walks, and traditional markets while learning about the region's agricultural heritage, industry, and vibrant community life throughout the year.
