Tabuk
Tabuk is the capital of the Tabuk Province and the largest city in the Northern Region. It serves as a gateway to historical sites and is renowned for its ancient castles, archaeological treasures, and strategic location on the historic pilgrimage and trade routes. The city features moderate climate, beautiful natural landscapes, and rich historical heritage dating back thousands of years.

Sakaka - Al-Jouf
Sakaka is the capital of Al-Jouf Province, known for its ancient civilization and vast olive groves. One of the oldest continuously inhabited areas in the Arabian Peninsula, Al-Jouf is home to numerous historical monuments and archaeological sites. The region is famous for producing high-quality olives and combines rich history with stunning natural beauty.

Arar
Arar is the capital of the Northern Borders Province, characterized by vast deserts and unique archaeological treasures. The city is home to authentic tribal heritage and ancient traditions. Arar contains important archaeological sites dating back thousands of years and features the stunning beauty of the Arabian desert, blending ancient history with modern development.

Qurayyat
Qurayyat is an important city in Al-Jouf Province, located near the Jordanian border. The city is known for its agricultural activities, particularly date palm cultivation and olive farming. Qurayyat features beautiful desert landscapes, historical sites, and serves as a commercial hub connecting Saudi Arabia with neighboring countries. The city combines traditional Bedouin culture with modern urban development.

Dumat al-Jandal
Dumat al-Jandal is a historic city in Al-Jouf Province, known for its ancient archaeological sites and significant historical importance. The city features the famous Marid Castle, one of the most prominent historical landmarks in the region, and ancient rock art dating back thousands of years. Dumat al-Jandal offers visitors a unique glimpse into the ancient history of the Arabian Peninsula.

Overview of the Northern Region

The Northern Region of Saudi Arabia is a region rich in history and heritage, extending from Al-Jouf in the south to the Northern Borders. This region is famous for its ancient historical sites, vast deserts, and authentic tribal heritage. The main cities in the region are known for being important stations on ancient trade and pilgrimage routes, containing unique archaeological treasures dating back thousands of years.
