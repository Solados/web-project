Eastern of Saudi Arabia
Dhahran
Dhahran is a calm and clean city. It is one of the most important cities in the Eastern Province because it is home to Saudi Aramco. It has organized residential areas, universities, and educational centers. The city feels peaceful and family-friendly. Visitors can explore museums, family parks, and nearby coastal attractions while learning about the region's oil industry history and community life.

Jubail
Jubail is a large city with two main parts: Jubail Industrial City and Jubail City (the older area). The industrial area is known for its large factories and well-planned layout, while the older area has calm beaches and a traditional coastal feel. Al-Ahsa
Al-Ahsa is one of the largest regions in Saudi Arabia, known for its huge oasis and many palm trees. It includes cities like Hofuf and Al-Mubarraz, and is known for its calm atmosphere and historic feel. It has nice places to visit such as Ibrahim Palace and Al-Qarah Mountain, mixing nature and tradition.
